US Budget Visualization
The US Budget, arguably the most important document for understanding what our government does, is completely inaccessable. This visualization fixes that. Click any year to see the budget for that year.

Click once on any cell to see a graph of how that budget item has changed over time, and double click to get more details about any specific item.
